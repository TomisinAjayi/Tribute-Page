Biography

"Kanye" Omari West, born in Atlanta, raised in Chicago.
He was known as a Producer in Roc-A-Fella Records and decided to pursuit a solo career as a rapper, he released "The College Dropout" and founded G.O.O.D Music Records in 2004. Along the line while creating amazing music, he is also known as a Fashion Designer, Politican and a great Husband and Father.
As a Fashion Designer, he collaborated with Nike, Louis Vuitton and A.P.C, his most remarkable collaboration was his Yeezy with Adidas in 2015. He is the Founder of DONDA, a Creative Content Company named after his mother. Kanye is also popularly known for receiving many awards in his music career and as a Fashion Designer, also as top 100 most influential people in the world. His interest in U.S Politics received the media's attention as he ran an Independent Presidential Campaign in 2020.
